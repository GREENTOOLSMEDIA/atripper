ATRIPPER — FANART

BetyAI — PauAi with the soul of Bettie Page

FANART is a special space within ATRIPPER. Here, the intention is not to produce a lot, but to deeply enjoy what I create.

BetyAI was born from PauAi and is inspired by Bettie Page, bringing her into this world that seems to get a little crazier every day.

The images draw from those classic magazine covers I used to see at the newsstands: Life, Vanity Fair, Esquire, Rolling Stone… Iconic, inspiring covers that made my life better and still remain a powerful reference for me.

Between 12 and 24 covers… or however many end up coming out. Maybe they’ll stop at 13, as a tribute to the movie Friday the 13th.

I make them when the inspiration hits. No rush. Sometimes with a coffee in hand, sometimes while smoking a cigarette, savoring every moment of the process… just like life used to be enjoyed.

ATRIPPER is not about quantity. It’s about reconnecting with that beautiful nostalgia — going back to the essence where one truly enjoyed what they were doing, moment by moment.
